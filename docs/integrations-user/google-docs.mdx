# Connect Google Docs

Easily link your Google Docs account to Company A to sync and manage your documents—all directly within your Company A workspace.

---

## Why Connect Google Docs?

Connecting Google Docs allows you to:
- Import Google Docs into Company A
- Export created docs or templates back to Google Docs
- Collaborate seamlessly between platforms

## How to Connect Your Google Docs Account

Connecting is quick and secure:

1. Go to the **Integrations** or **Connections** page in your Company A account.
2. Find **Google Docs** in the list and click **Connect**.
3. A Google login window will appear. Sign in with the Google account you’d like to connect.
4. Google will ask you to review permissions. Click **Allow** to proceed.
5. That’s it! Your Google Docs account is now connected.

<Callout type="info">
You can disconnect or switch accounts anytime from your Company A integration settings.
</Callout>

---

## What Permissions Are Requested?

When you connect, Company A requests limited permissions from your Google account to enable document syncing and collaboration.

**Requested permissions include:**

- **documents.currentonly**
  - _Access only to the currently open Google Docs document (most limited and secure scope)._ 

Depending on your use case and company settings, additional permissions may be requested:

- **documents**
  - _Full read/write access to all Google Docs (viewing, creating, editing, deleting documents)._
- **documents.readonly**
  - _View-only access to your Google Docs documents._
- **drive.file**
  - _Access only to files that Company A has created or you’ve specifically chosen to share._
- **drive.readonly**
  - _View-only access to all Google Drive files, including Docs._
- **drive**
  - _Full access to all files and folders in Google Drive, including Docs._

<Callout type="warning">
Company A requests only the minimum permissions required for syncing and collaboration. You can review or change these at any time in your Google account’s app permissions.
</Callout>

## Testing Your Connection

After connecting:
- Try importing a Google Doc into your Company A workspace, or
- Export a document from Company A to Google Docs.

If the sync works, your connection is active!

---

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

**I can’t see my Google Docs or get sync errors. What should I do?**
- Double-check that you are signed in to the correct Google account.
- Make sure you approved all necessary permissions during setup.
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration.

**The connection was working but now fails. Why?**
- Your permissions may have changed. Check your Google account’s security settings.
- Token expiry can require re-authorization. Simply reconnect to refresh access.
