# Connect Google Docs

Easily sync your Google Docs with Company A for seamless document collaboration and automation.

---

## Why connect Google Docs?

- Import Google Docs into Company A.
- Export Company A content to Google Docs.
- Automate workflows and stay organized across platforms.

---

## How to Connect Your Google Docs Account

Connecting is fast and secure. Here’s how:

1. Go to the **Integrations** or **Connections** page in your Company A account.
2. Find **Google Docs** in the list and click **Connect**.
3. A Google login window will appear. Sign in with your Google account.
4. Review the permissions requested, then click **Allow** to continue.
5. You’re done! Your Google Docs account is linked to Company A.

<Callout type="info">
You can disconnect or switch Google accounts anytime from the same Integrations page.
</Callout>

---

## What Permissions Are Requested?

When you connect Google Docs, Company A asks for the minimum required permissions from your Google account to perform sync and document management. Permissions may vary depending on the features you use.

**The typical permission requested:**

- **Access only the currently open Google Doc**  
  _Allows Company A temporary access to just the open document._  
  **Scope:** `documents.currentonly`

**Depending on your features, these may also appear:**

- **Full access to your Google Docs**  
  _Allows viewing, creating, editing, deleting all your Google Docs docs._  
  **Scope:** `documents`
- **Read-only access to your Google Docs**  
  _Lets Company A only view your docs and their info._  
  **Scope:** `documents.readonly`
- **Access to files you create/share with Company A**  
  _Lets Company A see only Docs/files it creates or you choose to share._  
  **Scope:** `drive.file`
- **Read-only access to all Google Drive files**  
  **Scope:** `drive.readonly`
- **Full access to your entire Google Drive**  
  **Scope:** `drive`

<Callout type="warning">
Company A will never request more access than is required for your selected workflows. You can review or revoke these permissions from your Google Account at any time.
</Callout>

## Testing Your Connection

To confirm the integration is working:
- Try importing a Google Doc into Company A.
- Or, export a Company A document to Google Docs.

Success means your connection is active!

---

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

**Can’t see your Google Docs or getting sync errors?**
- Ensure you connected the correct Google account.
- Check you allowed all requested permissions.
- Try disconnecting, then reconnecting Google Docs in Company A.

**The connection stopped working.**
- Your session could have expired, or permissions were revoked. Simply reconnect from Integrations.

Need help? Contact Company A’s support team or visit our Help Center.
